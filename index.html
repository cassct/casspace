Hi, I’m Cassandra Thomas, but you can call me Cass.

I’m a graphic designer from Kota Kinabalu, now based in Kuala Lumpur.

I thrive on creating unique designs that break the mold and leave a lasting impression. I’m always on the move, seeking new challenges and exploring different industries because that’s how I grow. I don’t settle for ordinary—I push boundaries to make my clients go “wow” because I believe great design should always be a little extra.

Fun fact: If you are wondering why Casspace, it is because this is where I step into my main character energy. It is my space to create, explore new styles, and bring unexpected ideas to life.
